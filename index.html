WELCOME TO OUR UNIVERSITY

Chandigarh University (CU) is a leading Indian Institution offering its students a unique amlagamation of professional and academic excellence.The University has been accredited with the prestigious A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accrediation Council (NAAC). Chandigarh University has become Youngest and the only private university in India to bag an A+ grade in the first cycle of the accreditation process and has also become the only state private university of Punjab to be accredited by NAAC.
